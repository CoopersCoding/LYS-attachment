LAST YEAR SINGLE ATTACHMENT SURVEY RESULTS

Our recommendation to you is to stick with the first results the quiz reveals to you.

Anxious Attachment Style
If you received this Path recommendation, your score indicates that the area where you may be currently stuck in is your relationship with God. Following the Relationship with God path is an invitation to strengthen your relationship with God. Your relationship with the Father is foundational for all other relationships.

On this path you will learn how to:

• Hear God more clearly and more often
• Stop striving to be accepted by God and start thriving in His unconditional love
• Overcome orphan or poverty mentalities that are holding you back from an abundant life
• Get free from religious mindsets like condemnation, guilt, and fear and live in the finished work of the cross.
• Rebuild your identity as a son/daughter of God so you can fulfill the good plans God has for your life.
• Understand the character of God and who He is.

Secure Attachment Style
If you received this Path recommendation, your score indicates that the area where you may be currently stuck in is your relationship with yourself.

Following the Relationship with Self path is an invitation to grow in confidence in who you are and how you represent yourself to the world.

On this path you will grow in the following areas:

• Become more self-aware and self-accepting
• Learn how to be kind to yourself and silence your inner critic
• Cultivate a healthy self image
• Cultivate a healthy weight/BMI
• Learn about your unique personality and how to optimize it for better relationships
• Create a personal brand
1. Learn how to represent your best self to the world
2. Learn how to communicate
3. Learn how to dress
4. Hone your personal style
• Prioritize self-care both physically and mentally to build a healthier and happier you

Avoidant Attachment Style
If you received this Path recommendation, your score indicates that the area where you may be currently stuck in is your relationship with others.

Following the Relationship with Others path is an invitation to grow in connection with others so you can live an interdependent and abundant life.

On this path you will grow in the following areas:

• Strengthen your boundaries to attract higher quality people
• Cultivate your communication skills so you can be confident talking with anyone
• Heal relationships with family and friends
• Learn how to create meaningful connections with others
• Overcome jealousy, comparison, and judgment
• Get to the root of people pleasing to prevent burnout and resentment
